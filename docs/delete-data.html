Delete Your Data

If you wish to disconnect your TikTok account or request deletion of your imported data, email:

hello@northstar-creator.com

Users may disconnect their TikTok account at any time from within NorthStar. Once disconnected, NorthStar will no longer access new TikTok data unless the user reconnects and authorizes access again.

Please Include

  • TikTok username
  • Email used with NorthStar
  • Request type: disconnect or delete

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.
