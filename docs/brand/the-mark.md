## Meaning

Northstar exists to help creator data become clear direction. The Mark‚ĄĘ combines two symbols:

- The Creator Compass‚ĄĘ
- The Spark‚ĄĘ

Together they represent the moment a creator stops staring at raw signals and knows what to do next.

## The Creator Compass‚ĄĘ

The Creator Compass‚ĄĘ is a minimal four-point compass.

The four points represent the four creator questions:

- North: What should I create?
- East: What should I sell?
- South: What should I improve?
- West: What should I stop doing?

The north point is slightly longer than the other points. This gives the mark direction without making it feel nautical, vintage, ornate, or like generic compass clipart.

## The Spark‚ĄĘ

The Spark‚ĄĘ is the center element.

It uses two overlapping four-point stars:

- A warm gold outer star
- A bright highlight star
- A subtle glow where the two overlap

The Spark‚ĄĘ represents the exact moment data becomes direction. It should feel rare and meaningful, not decorative.

## Clear Space

Keep clear space around The Mark‚ĄĘ equal to at least one quarter of the mark width.

For example, if the mark is 64px wide, keep at least 16px of open space around it.

Do not place The Mark‚ĄĘ inside crowded UI, directly against text, or over complex imagery.

## Minimum Size

- Standard mark: 24px minimum width
- Horizontal logo: 160px minimum width
- App icon: 32px minimum width
- Favicon: use `favicon.svg`

At very small sizes, use the favicon because it removes the outer ring and simplifies the compass.

## Incorrect Usage

Do not:

- Rotate the mark
- Stretch or skew the mark
- Add extra colors
- Replace the Spark‚ĄĘ with a generic star
- Use nautical, vintage, ornate, or decorative compass styling
- Add heavy shadows
- Animate the mark repeatedly
- Place the gold Spark‚ĄĘ on a busy gold background

## Animation

Use the optional CSS class `spark-pulse` only when a meaningful new Signal appears.

Animation rules:

- Pulse once
- Keep it subtle
- Do not loop by default
- Do not use the animation as decoration

The Spark‚ĄĘ should remain special. It is for moments when Northstar has direction worth noticing.
