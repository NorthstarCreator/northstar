Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

NorthStar Creator is designed as a creator analytics and planning platform. The current local version stores creator business data on the user's own device unless the user intentionally exports, imports, syncs, or connects an external service.

Information Northstar May Store Locally

NorthStar may store creator data such as products, videos, sample records, sales records, notes, hooks, generated plans, settings, and backup metadata in local browser storage, bundled local files, or approved connected systems.

How Data Is Used

User data is used only to provide creator dashboards, analytics, planning tools, and product features. NorthStar does not sell, rent, or share creator data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Connections

When TikTok authentication is enabled, OAuth access tokens will be handled through a secure server-side connector using industry-standard practices. Access tokens and refresh tokens will not be stored in browser localStorage. NorthStar will only access TikTok data after a user explicitly authorizes the connection through TikTok's official OAuth flow.

TikTok Data

When a user chooses to connect a TikTok account, NorthStar accesses only the data authorized through TikTok's official OAuth process and only the permissions approved by the user. This information is used solely to provide creator dashboard features, analytics, organization tools, and planning insights within NorthStar.

Backups and Exports

Users may export backups from Northstar. Exported files are controlled by the user and should be stored carefully.

Data Deletion

Users may disconnect their TikTok account at any time. Users may also request deletion of imported data by contacting hello@northstar-creator.com. Upon request, NorthStar will delete applicable stored account data in accordance with applicable laws and platform requirements.

Contact

Users may request deletion or contact support through the Contact page or by emailing hello@northstar-creator.com.
