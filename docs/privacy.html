Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Northstar is designed as a local creator operating system. The current local version stores creator business data on the user's own device unless the user intentionally exports, imports, syncs, or connects an external service.

Information Northstar May Store Locally

Northstar may store products, videos, sample records, sales records, notes, hooks, generated video plans, settings, and backup metadata in local browser storage or bundled local files.

Third-Party Connections

If Northstar connects to TikTok or another platform in the future, authorization should happen through the platform's official flow and a secure connector. Northstar should not store access tokens or refresh tokens in browser localStorage.

Backups and Exports

Users may export backups from Northstar. Exported files are controlled by the user and should be stored carefully.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the Contact page.
