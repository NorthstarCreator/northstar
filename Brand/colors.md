# Northstar Colors

## Core

- Background: `#FBFAF7`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Ink: `#111827`
- Muted text: `#6B7280`
- Border: `rgba(17, 24, 39, 0.08)`

## Workspace Accents

- Raised Right: warm red `#C9453F`, gold `#D99A22`
- Truth Tuned Tribe: blue `#2F6FD6`, teal `#0F9F9A`

## Status

- Success: `#2F9E62`
- Watch / Wait: `#D69226`
- Retire / Risk: `#B85B5B`

Use accent colors as signals, not backgrounds. The interface should stay mostly white, ivory, and calm.
